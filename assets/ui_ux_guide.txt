UI/UX Guidelines for Checkout Page

1. Color Scheme
   - Primary Button (Pay Now): Green (#008000 or similar).
   - Secondary Buttons (Add to Cart, Apply): Default browser style or neutral.
   - Error Messages: Red text, displayed inline below the invalid field.
   - Success Messages: Green text, bold.

2. Form Validation
   - Name: Required field.
   - Email: Required field, must contain '@'.
   - Address: Required field.
   - Validation should trigger on 'Submit' (Pay Now click).

3. Layout
   - Products list at the top.
   - Cart summary in the middle or side.
   - Checkout form (User details, Shipping, Payment) at the bottom.
