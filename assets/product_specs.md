# Product Specifications - E-Shop Checkout

## Discount Codes
- **SAVE15**: Applies a 15% discount to the total order value (including shipping).
- **WELCOME10**: Applies a $10 discount (fixed amount). *Note: Currently only SAVE15 is implemented in the frontend logic for this sprint.*

## Shipping Policies
- **Standard Shipping**: Free of charge. Delivery in 5-7 business days.
- **Express Shipping**: Flat rate of $10. Delivery in 1-2 business days.

## Cart Logic
- Users can add multiple items.
- The total price updates dynamically when items are added, shipping is changed, or discounts are applied.
- Minimum order value: None.

## Payment Methods
- Credit Card
- PayPal
